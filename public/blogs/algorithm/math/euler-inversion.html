    # 利用

    化简 ∑i=1n∑j=1m(i,j)\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m(i,j)i=1∑n​j=1∑m​(i,j)

    ∑i=1n∑j=1m(i,j)=∑i=1n∑j=1m∑d∣(i,j)ϕ(d)=∑d=1min(n,m)ϕ(d)∑i=1n∑j=1m[d∣i∧d∣j]=∑d=1min(n,m)ϕ(d)⌊nd⌋⌊md⌋\begin{aligned} &\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m(i,j)\\ =&\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m\sum\limits_{d|(i,j)}\phi(d)\\ =&\sum\limits_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}\phi(d)\sum\limits_{i=1}^n\sum\limits_{j=1}^m[d|i\wedge d|j]\\ =&\sum\limits_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}\phi(d)\left\lfloor\frac nd\right\rfloor\left\lfloor\frac md\right\rfloor \end{aligned}===​i=1∑n​j=1∑m​(i,j)i=1∑n​j=1∑m​d∣(i,j)∑​ϕ(d)d=1∑min(n,m)​ϕ(d)i=1∑n​j=1∑m​[d∣i∧d∣j]d=1∑min(n,m)​ϕ(d)⌊dn​⌋⌊dm​⌋​
